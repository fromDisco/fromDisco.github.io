GOOGLE DEVELOPER SCHOLARSHIP

I studied Print Design. But even I loved it, i realized, that print Design is not the kind of thing i want to do. Nevertheless i have fun creating things that are useful.

So I started to learn Frontend-Development. I am a bloody beginner. But I want to grow my skills. It's nice to see things work after you struggled to get them to work. And I love to see the result of my work, at the end of the day, the week, the project ... Developing makes this possible. Coding, in my eyes, is one of the most fascinating crafts of the 21th century.

And I really want to be a part of this. But I am always driven by perfection. And I think this scholarship can help me to get close to that.
